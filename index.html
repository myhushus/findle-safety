Findle – Child Safety & Reporting Policy

Findle strictly prohibits any content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to complying with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including reporting obligations to local or national authorities where required by law.

If CSAE-related content is detected or reported, Findle will take immediate action, including:

  • Removing the content or suspending the account.
  • Reporting the incident to relevant national or regional authorities.

Users may report any child safety concerns directly within the app via the Settings → Report option, which opens your email client to contact us at: director@findle.me

Alternatively, concerns can also be reported to the following external organizations:

  • NCMEC CyberTipline (USA)
  • INHOPE International Reporting

We take all reports seriously and will respond without delay. Thank you for helping us keep the Findle community safe.
